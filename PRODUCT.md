# CVLink product context

CVLink is a product UI for finding, reviewing and shortlisting professional talent from Cabo Verde and the PALOP region.

The first audience is recruiters, founders, consultancies and project leads who need to identify trustworthy professionals for African and lusophone projects. The second audience is professionals who want a credible public profile without exposing sensitive contact data by default.

The product must feel practical, verified and sober. It is not a European job board. It is a talent repository with a Cabo Verde first focus and a PALOP expansion path. The interface should help users finish tasks quickly: search, filter, compare, open a profile, save a search and shortlist talent.

Register: product.

Design principles:

- Trust before decoration. Verification, privacy and profile quality must be visible without making the interface noisy.
- Search is the main workflow. Filters must be easy to scan, easy to reset and usable on mobile.
- Recruiters need density. Cards may show several signals, but the primary scan path must stay clear: name, role, availability, location, skills and next action.
- Professionals must look credible. Profile photos and CV indicators should support confidence, not dominate the page.
- Use Portuguese for the interface. Text must render with correct accents and no encoding artefacts.

Anti-references:

- Generic SaaS dashboards with purple gradients.
- Over-rounded cards and decorative glass effects.
- Landing-page typography inside app screens.
- Repeated cards with equal weight everywhere.
- European positioning when the product is for Cabo Verde and PALOP talent.

Accessibility requirements:

- Body text must meet WCAG AA contrast.
- Touch targets must be at least 44px.
- Keyboard focus must be visible.
- The layout must not create horizontal scrolling on mobile.
